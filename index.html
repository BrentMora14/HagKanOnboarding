Point Persons

  • Point Persons are chosen/delegated members who will be included in Head-level communication channels for:
    • 1) Faster dissemination of information
    • 2) Better intercommittee communication
  • Point Persons will NOT have any added workload (aside from their existing member-level workload), they will simply have access to more channels
Point Persons serve as bridges between committees, ensuring seamless information flow without additional responsibilities.
